    North Texas is an above-average public university located in Denton, Texas in the Dallas-Fort Worth Area. It is a large institution with a full-time enrollment of 27,922 undergraduate students and a part-time enrollment of 5,936 undergraduate students. Admissions is somewhat competitive as the North Texas acceptance rate is 72%. Popular majors include Liberal Arts and Humanities, Psychology, and Biology. North Texas graduates 60% of its students.
