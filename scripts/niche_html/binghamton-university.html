    Binghamton University is known for having a very active party scene where drinking is quite common among students. Greek life plays a significant role on campus, offering both social connections and opportunities for volunteer work, although there have been some scandals associated with it. For students who enjoy nightlife, downtown Binghamton and various bars provide many options, though some students find the bar scene mediocre and the presence of younger, often intoxicated students tiring.

    What Students Say

    Binghamton University, SUNY, stands out for its rigorous academics, particularly in STEM and business programs, which many students praise for their challenge and the quality of education received, often deeming it a "Public Ivy." The campus fosters a vibrant social life with numerous clubs, organizations, and events, although some students feel more could be done to enhance community engagement, particularly for commuters. The dining options receive mixed feedback, with some students calling for improvements in food variety. Professors are generally seen as supportive and engaged, contributing to a collaborative learning environment. While the beautiful campus and community feel are frequently mentioned, some students have expressed a desire for better communication from administration and improvements to campus facilities. Overall, many students find Binghamton to be a valuable choice, balancing quality education with a lively campus experience.
