    South Florida is a highly rated public university located in Tampa, Florida. It is a large institution with a full-time enrollment of 30,184 undergraduate students and a part-time enrollment of 7,085 undergraduate students. Admissions is fairly competitive as the South Florida acceptance rate is 41%. Popular majors include Health Service Preparatory Studies, Psychology, and Biomedical Sciences and Molecular Medicine. South Florida graduates 75% of its students.

    What Students Say

    The University of South Florida (USF) receives praise for its supportive faculty and diverse academic offerings, making it a solid choice for many students. Reviewers appreciate the helpfulness of professors and academic advisors, often noting that they genuinely care about student success. The campus is described as beautiful and vibrant, with plenty of opportunities for social engagement, clubs, and activities, contributing to a sense of community. However, some students mention challenges with parking and advising processes, alongside mixed experiences regarding campus life and food options. Overall, USF stands out for its combination of strong academics, a lively campus atmosphere, and a commitment to student services, although some aspects, like navigating administrative matters, could be improved.
